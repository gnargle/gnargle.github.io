Cootie's X-Ray Vision - ROMP

In or before 2001

The world was a simpler place pre-9/11. All one needed to enjoy one's self was a flash to pose the question: what if you had x-ray specs? Wouldn't it be great to see a titty without having to take any clothes off?

There's not much to this 'game,' if you can even call it that. You take the glasses from the extremely racist traveller stereotype and use your cursor to reveal BOOBIES and WILLIES and MINGE. Then you reach the end of the 4 or so screens, float your cursor over the traveller's own scrote, and get stabbed. Game over.

The masking is pretty clever. Not sure if early flash was capable of simple mask blending or if this is some clever hack the developer put together. Probably the former, really.

2001 isn't all sunshine and roses, though. The racism is aforementioned. There's also some textbook fatphobia (because who would ever want to look at a fat person naked, am I right lads????) and of course, the requisite trans stereotype (oh my god, a woman, but she's bald under the wig and has a dick! Scandal! She has my number). It's hard to get mad at - this was probably made by a 13 year old boy trying to be edgy - but it is a useful time capsule of how far we've come in general acceptance of marginalised groups? The nearest neighbour for flash hentai now is artists posting their 18+ drawings on bluesky and in those circles you're likely to find some absolutely scrumptious portrayals of trans people, fat people, people of colour, et al. Porn is no longer a scene for boys and boys alone, and the world is better for it.
